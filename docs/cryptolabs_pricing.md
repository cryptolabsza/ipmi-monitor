## AI Token Usage

AI tokens are consumed when using:
- Fleet health summaries
- Maintenance task generation
- Predictive analytics
- Root cause analysis
- AI chat conversations

**Token Expiration:**
- All tokens expire 60 days after being granted
- Unused tokens are removed at expiration
- New tokens are added on subscription renewal

**Low Balance Warnings:**
- Email sent when balance reaches 10%
- Frozen when balance depleted
- Top up anytime with token packages

## FAQ

**Q: Can I try before I buy?**
A: Yes! Start a free 30-day trial with 1M tokens, no credit card required.

**Q: What happens when my trial ends?**
A: You'll be moved to the Free tier. Subscribe to keep AI features.

**Q: Can I add more servers later?**
A: Yes! Use the slider on your account page to adjust server count anytime.

**Q: What's the sweet spot at 50 servers?**
A: At exactly 50 servers, you get a $10 discount AND double the tokens (1M instead of 500K). It's the best value!

**Q: Should I get Professional or Standard+ at 400 servers?**
A: Professional is recommended. For only $25 more, you get 100 extra servers and 5.5M more tokens.

**Q: Do unused tokens roll over?**
A: No, tokens expire 60 days after being granted. Use them or lose them!

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: We don't offer refunds for partial months, but you keep access until the billing period ends.

**Q: Is there a discount for annual billing?**
A: Contact sales for annual billing discounts on Enterprise plans.
